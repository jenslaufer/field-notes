# AI Marketing Masterclass: beginner to expert in 60 minutes

> The Boring Marketer builds a **complete marketing system live in Claude Code in
> ~60 minutes** — positioning, landing page, lead magnet, interactive audit tool,
> SEO content, and a video ad. The stack: **Perplexity + Firecrawl + Playwright
> MCPs for research, ~17 hand-built skills for execution.** Big claim: Claude Code
> is what makes "vibe marketing" actually real — your marketing lives in the same
> terminal as your product. He's spent **$100M+ on ads**; this is how he launches now.

## The method: research → skills → build
- **Most people fail by prompting cold** and getting slop. The fix is upfront
  research — often ~1 hour before generating anything.
- **3 research layers:** Perplexity MCP (market/competitor/gap research) → Firecrawl
  (scrape sites; new **Firecrawl Agent** does research tasks) → Playwright MCP
  (open competitor sites, screenshot for inspo — open source, free).
- **Then skills.** Skills = "instruction manuals for your agent" — marketing
  frameworks trained deeply on one task. He built **17** he uses daily.
- **Keep it simple.** He cut his MCPs down to **Perplexity, Firecrawl, Playwright.**
- **Tool set:** Cursor/VS Code, Claude Code, WhisperFlow/Narrate for voice,
  $200/mo Claude Max ("never run out of tokens").

## The live build (boring local-business marketing agency)
1. **Positioning angles skill** → on "boring businesses doing $2–10M" produced
   angles: the anti-agency, the speed weapon, the unsexy gold mine. Winner:
   **"Boring Money"** — "from chasing work to selecting work; weeks of work in days."
2. **Task-based sub-agent** as a checkpoint — spin a fresh context window with a
   specialized role (e.g. "you're Greg, pick the best angle") for better results.
3. **Direct-response copy skill** — trained on Eugene Schwartz, Claude Hopkins,
   updated for today. Generated the landing-page copy + a founder-story section.
4. **Playwright MCP** → screenshot a real competitor (Scorpion) for intelligence.
5. **Front-end design skill (Anthropic)** → a conversion landing page with an
   *anti-AI* aesthetic — no purple gradients, rounded corners, or emoji slop.
6. **Orchestrator skill** → "I don't know what to do next." It maps what you have
   (positioning, voice, page) vs. missing (lead magnet, traffic) and picks the
   next skill itself.
7. **Lead-magnet skill** → 3–5 concepts (ROI calculator, exposé PDF, **5-minute
   marketing audit**). Chose the audit — "make it a tool, perceived value goes up."
8. **Interactive audit** built as a modal — 15 questions, instant score, gated
   report that builds the email list (replaces a paid Typeform/Tally).
9. **Keyword-research + SEO-content skills** → programmatic-SEO pages for local
   markets (e.g. "HVAC marketing Phoenix") with the lead magnet embedded.
10. **Remotion (video-in-Claude-Code)** → a branded animated video ad, multiple
    sizes (landscape/story/square), **$0 cost**. One prompt; ping-pong ~30 min to
    polish. Image asset made in Nano Banana Pro, bg removed in Canva.

## On skills (his strongest opinion)
- **Underrated, but not equal.** Generic skills get you 80% there; the last 10–20%
  of **expert taste** can't be AI-generated — download *your* brain into a skill.
- **Build your own:** deep-research a topic with Perplexity/Firecrawl, then "Claude,
  create a skill from this," add world-class references, hand-review for taste.
- **Free skill directories exist** — you don't have to build all your own.

## Marketing tips
- **Make lead magnets without writing an ebook:** record yourself working for 2
  hours, transcribe, let Claude Code extract it into a playbook/guide.
- **One angle = one landing page.** Generate a *separate* dialed page per ICP
  (agencies, cost-savers, DIY founders) → higher conversion than one generic page.
  "You could make 100 landing pages and 100 ads — become a testing machine."
- **Steal ad frameworks from DTC e-com** (highest volume/spend, best hooks) and
  repurpose for boring/B2B/education products. Mine the Facebook Ad Library.
- **Statics over AI-UGC for now:** extract winning frameworks, generate a few
  statics with Nano Banana Pro (via Glyph MCP); Remotion for product-demo videos.
- **Even if you'll hire an agency, build it yourself first** — you'll understand and
  dial in your own funnel.

## Why this matters for us
- **A full, copyable launch-kit funnel in one terminal:** positioning → landing page
  → gated audit tool → SEO content → ad — exactly our lead-funnel + email stack.
- **The interactive-audit lead magnet** (quiz → score → gated report) is a
  high-converting, build-it-once pattern we can reuse across niches.
- **Research-first (Perplexity/Firecrawl/Playwright) beats prompt-and-pray** — a
  guardrail against AI slop in anything we generate.
- **Skills = encoded taste:** our domain expertise becomes a reusable, on-brand
  asset rather than a one-off prompt.
