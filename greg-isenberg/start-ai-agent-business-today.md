# Start an AI Agent Business Today

> Solo episode: seven tiny, cash-flowing businesses you can build in a few
> prompts with **Genspark Claw** (Genspark's cloud agent running **Sonnet 4.6**,
> ~$25/mo, 4-min setup). Greg ships two live (dead-domain flipper, restaurant
> liquidation broker), builds a third on camera (hiring-signal cold outreach),
> and hands over a **5-step idea framework**. Goal: a **$200–$1,500/day** business
> with AI as your employee. Tiny and boring beats billion-dollar.

## Setup: treat the agent like an employee

- **Genspark Claw = AI employee in Slack.** Configure it inside Slack so it lives
  where you already work — a remote PM + vibe coder in one.
- **Downloadable skills + local file access.** Pull in skills; give it files to
  act on. ~4-minute setup.
- **Keep it always-on.** Use heartbeat / prevent-sleep so it runs unattended like
  a real hire. Talk to it in plain English; it ships fixes when bugs appear.

## The 7 ideas

### 1. Dead Domain Flipper
- **Point it at expired domains** — GoDaddy auctions, DropCatch, expired lists.
- **Clean criteria:** DR 20+, no adult/gambling history, niche keywords, under budget.
- **Output:** ranked picks under your budget, every morning. Flip the spread.

### 2. Local Restaurant Liquidation Broker
- **Monitor closures + auctions:** BizBuySell, AuctionZip, BidSpotter, Craigslist,
  Florida Bankruptcy Court.
- **Build comps** across 40+ equipment types with **live eBay sold-price lookups**.
- **Surface deal cards with 300%+ spreads.** Broker estate → next operator for a
  **15–30% fee, zero inventory risk**.

### 3. Hiring-Signal Cold Outreach (built live)
- **Thesis:** hiring = budget. Monitor job boards daily for hiring signals.
- **It scraped 222 jobs** across Hacker News "Who's Hiring," Remotive, Greenhouse.
- **Scored them → found 14 marketing-leader hires** → enriched with LinkedIn
  decision-makers → drafted a personalized cold email referencing each post.
- **Self-healing:** a bug shipped HTML entities into drafts; Greg just told it,
  and it shipped the fix.

### 4. Buy-or-Build Memos
- **Point Claw at BizBuySell or Acquire.com** → generate "should-I-even-call"
  acquisition memos in **~6 minutes**.

### 5. Dead Product Hunt SEO
- **Scan Product Hunt launches from 2–4 years ago** for dead sites that still
  hold live SEO traffic. Relaunch or redirect the equity.

### 6. Forgotten Apps
- **Hunt App Store rankings** for ex-top-100 apps now sitting at 500+ that still
  hold tens of thousands of reviews. Acquire and revive.

### 7. (Pattern reuse)
- Each idea follows the same **flip, broker, or relaunch** motion against a
  mispriced asset.

## The 5-step idea framework

1. **Find a messy public feed** (auctions, job boards, app charts).
2. **Identify a mispriced asset** inside it.
3. **Look for a trigger event** (closure, layoff, hiring spree, ranking drop).
4. **Name an obvious buyer with money.**
5. **Pick a liquidity point** — flip, broker, retainer, or relaunch.

### Three hunting lenses
- **Places of constant change** — where prices and listings churn daily.
- **Things people ignore** — neglected assets nobody is watching.
- **Clear urgency + spread** — assets with a deadline and a price gap.

## Marketing tips

- **Outreach is the product in Idea 3:** the agent doesn't just find leads, it
  writes a **personalized email referencing each prospect's own job post** —
  relevance manufactured at scale (14 tailored emails from 222 scraped jobs).
- **Trigger-event targeting beats spray-and-pray** — sell into companies *at the
  moment* budget appears (a fresh hire, a closure, a funding event).
- **Tiny + boring + cash-flowing** is the distribution edge: niches too small for
  VCs have no competition and a clear, reachable buyer.
- **One agent, one feed, one buyer** keeps the funnel legible — easy to measure
  and easy to scale by cloning the pattern to a new vertical.

## Why this matters for us

- The **Feed → Asset → Trigger → Buyer → Monetization** framework is a reusable
  idea-validation template for launch-kit — map any niche to a monetizable motion.
- **Trigger-based cold outreach** (hiring signals) is a copyable lead-funnel
  recipe: scrape public feeds, score, enrich, personalize, send.
- **Agent-as-employee in Slack at ~$25/mo** is a cheap, concrete way to automate
  our own lead research and content ops.
- Reinforces **distribution-first**: the asset is cheap, the edge is the
  buyer-at-the-right-moment.
